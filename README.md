# Hotel Booking Analytics – Business Metrics Documentation

This document explains the key business metrics used in the Hotel Booking Analytics Dashboard.

## Revenue
Revenue is the total income earned by the hotel from room bookings during a specific time period.

## Total Bookings
Total number of reservations made by customers, including checked-out, cancelled, and no-show bookings.

## Total Capacity
Total number of rooms available for sale across all hotels during a selected period.

## Total Successful Bookings
Number of bookings that were successfully completed (customers stayed and used the room).

## Occupancy %
Percentage of available rooms that were actually occupied.  
It measures how efficiently the hotel fills its rooms.

## Average Rating
Average rating given by customers based on their stay experience.

## Number of Days
Total number of days included in the selected reporting period.

## Total Cancelled Bookings
Total number of bookings cancelled by customers before check-in.

## Cancellation %
Percentage of total bookings that were cancelled.

## Total Checked Out
Number of bookings where customers successfully completed their stay.

## Total No-Show Bookings
Number of customers who booked a room but neither cancelled nor showed up.

## No Show Rate %
Percentage of total bookings where customers did not arrive without cancelling.

## Booking % by Platform
Percentage contribution of each booking platform to total bookings.

## Booking % by Room Class
Percentage contribution of each room category to total bookings.

## ADR (Average Daily Rate)
Average revenue earned per room sold during a specific period.

## Realisation %
Percentage of bookings that resulted in actual stays.

## RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room)
Average revenue generated per available room, whether occupied or not.

## DBRN (Daily Booked Room Nights)
Average number of rooms booked per day.

## DSRN (Daily Sellable Room Nights)
Average number of rooms available for sale per day.

## DURN (Daily Utilized Room Nights)
Average number of rooms actually used by customers per day.

## Revenue WoW Change %
Percentage increase or decrease in revenue compared to the previous week.

## Occupancy WoW Change %
Percentage change in occupancy rate compared to the previous week.

## ADR WoW Change %
Percentage change in ADR compared to the previous week.

## RevPAR WoW Change %
Percentage change in RevPAR compared to the previous week.

## Realisation WoW Change %
Percentage change in realisation rate compared to the previous week.
